MightyTask

Text expansion & automation for macOS

macOS

MightyTask is a powerful text expansion and automation tool that lets you create snippets, record macros, and automate repetitive typing across any application on your Mac. Type a short abbreviation and watch it expand into full paragraphs, formatted text, or dynamic content — saving you hours of repetitive work.

Key Features

Text Expansion

Create custom abbreviations that instantly expand into full text snippets. Type a few characters and let MightyTask do the rest.

Dynamic Variables

Insert live data into your snippets — dates, times, clipboard contents, mathematical calculations, and more.

Fill-In Forms

Add interactive fields to your snippets that prompt you for input on each expansion — perfect for templates and personalized responses.

Macro Recording

Record keyboard and mouse actions, then replay them on demand. Automate complex multi-step workflows with a single trigger.

App-Specific Snippets

Assign snippets to specific applications so they only activate where you need them — keeping your workflow organized and conflict-free.

Rich Text Support

Expand formatted text with HTML and RTF support. Insert styled content, links, and structured layouts directly into any app.

And More

Sentry Mode

Keep your Mac awake and active with automated cursor movement — perfect for long-running tasks.

Sound Feedback

Hear a confirmation sound when snippets expand. Choose from built-in sounds or add your own.

Statistics Tracking

Monitor how often you use each snippet and how much time you've saved with detailed usage stats.

Import & Export

Migrate your snippets from TextExpander, Alfred, or aText. Export and back up your collection anytime.

Snippet Packs

Bundle and share collections of snippets with your team or the community.

Snippet Organization

Organize snippets into categories, search by name or content, and keep everything tidy as your collection grows.
