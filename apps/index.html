Our Apps

Tools built to streamline your workflow and boost productivity.

MightyTask is a powerful text expansion and automation tool for macOS. Create snippets with custom abbreviations that instantly expand into full text — including dynamic variables, fill-in forms, and rich formatting. Record and replay keyboard and mouse macros, organize everything into categories, and import snippets from other tools. Works system-wide across every app on your Mac.

Key Features

Text Expansion

Type a short abbreviation and it instantly expands into full text. Supports delimiter-triggered (space, tab, enter) and exact-match expansion with case-insensitive matching.

Dynamic Variables

Insert live data with variables like {{date}}, {{time}}, {{clipboard}}, {{username}}, {{uuid}}, {{random:1:100}}, {{calc:2+2}}, and {{cursor}} for cursor positioning after expansion.

Fill-In Forms

Add interactive fields with {{field:Name}} or {{field:Name:Default}}. A form pops up on expansion so you can fill in custom values before the text is inserted — ideal for templates and email replies.

Macro Recording

Record full keyboard and mouse sequences — clicks, movement, and keystrokes with precise timing. Save recordings, load them later, and replay with configurable repeat counts.

App-Specific Snippets

Control exactly where each snippet works. Use whitelist mode to limit a snippet to specific apps, or blacklist mode to disable it in certain apps. Automatically skips secure password fields.

Rich Text Support

Create snippets with full formatting using HTML or RTF. MightyTask automatically converts HTML to rich text and pastes it with styles, links, and layouts preserved in any app.

And More

Sentry Mode

Prevent your Mac from sleeping with automated cursor movement at configurable intervals from 1–10 seconds. Toggle it on and off from the main window or a global shortcut.

Sound Feedback

Hear an audio confirmation each time a snippet expands. Choose from 9 built-in macOS sounds or add your own custom MP3, AIFF, WAV, or M4A files. Preview sounds before selecting.

Statistics & Usage Tracking

Track how many times each snippet is used and when it was last expanded. View totals, most-used rankings, and category breakdowns. Export your stats to CSV.

Import & Export

Smart import automatically detects and converts snippets from TextExpander, Alfred, and aText. Export your collection as JSON or CSV for backups and sharing.

Snippet Packs

Bundle snippets into shareable packs with author, description, and version metadata. Export as .mightypack files to share with your team or the community.

Organization & Search

Organize snippets into custom categories, search by abbreviation or content, multi-select for batch export or delete, and duplicate snippets with one click.

Global Shortcuts

Control everything from the keyboard. Dedicated hotkeys for recording (Cmd+1–4), Sentry Mode (Cmd+Shift+S), new snippets, search, and more — all without leaving your current app.

Automatic Backups

Your snippets are automatically backed up before every change. MightyTask keeps the last 10 timestamped backups so your data is always safe.
